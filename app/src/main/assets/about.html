KVK Namakkal

ICAR - Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Namakkal is a peripheral unit of Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University, Chennai with funding of Indian Council of Agricultural Research, New Delhi functioning since 13.10.2004.

TANUVAS

Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (TANUVAS) is a veterinary university founded in 1989 in Madhavaram Milk Colony, Chennai, India. It is composed of the Veterinary College and Research Institutes, Veterinary University Training and Research Centres, Farmers Training Centres to educate the students to become eminent Veterinary doctors, and also serve the farming communities for their sustainable livelihood through livestock farming.

ICAR

The Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) is an autonomous organisation under the Department of Agricultural Research and Education (DARE), Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of India. Formerly known as Imperial Council of Agricultural Research, it was established on 16 July 1929 as a registered society under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 in pursuance of the report of the Royal Commission on Agriculture. The ICAR has its headquarters at New Delhi. The Council is the apex body for co-ordinating, guiding and managing research and education in agriculture including horticulture, fisheries and animal sciences in the entire country. With 101 ICAR institutes and 71 agricultural universities spread across the country, this is one of the largest national agricultural systems in the world.

Activities

Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Namakkal, a centre for transfer of technologies from lab to various groups like Farming Community, Self Help Groups, Rural Youths, Extension Personnel etc., through organizing various events like Trainings, On Farm Testings, Front Line Demonstrations and Advisory services regularly.

To impart the scientific skills and knowledge among the farm women, farmers & physically challenged people and to adopt the scientific technologies in their farms, KVK is conducting various training programmes, awareness campaigns, exhibitions, demonstrations, field days and camps in the following the subjects as follows:

  • Agriculture
  • Horticulture
  • Agricultural Entomology
  • Soil Science
  • Animal Science
  • Fisheries

Training Programmes

KVK Namakkal is conducting training programmes on Agriculture, Horticulture crop production and Livestock rearing. Institutional units / Demo units like Livestock, Fish rearing unit, Vermicompost unit, Azolla unit, Honey bee unit, Shadenet nursery production unit, Coconut sapling unit, Bio control agents production unit, Compost unit, Mushroom shed are developed and maintained in KVK for the benefit of farming community. Also KVK Namakkal is conducting 200 hours skill training programmes regularly in topics like Poultry, Piggery, Mushroom grower, Honeybee grower, Organic grower, and Soil & Water Testing Lab Assistant.

Soil and Water Testing Laboratory is in operation since September 2011. In the last 5 years 6210 soil health cards and 640 water health cards were issued to farmers of Namakkal district. Processing machineries were distributed to five KVK farmer groups to motivate them to improve the production, processing and value addition of minor millets.

Climate Resilient Agriculture (CRA)

It is an approach that includes sustainably using existing natural resources through crop and livestock production systems to achieve long-term higher productivity and farm incomes under climate variabilities. KVK, Namakkal has introduced many successful climate resilient interventions for sustainable agriculture in Namakkal district through National Innovations on Climate Resilient Agriculture (NICRA) from 2011-2020.
