C. Contingency Measures

  • Imbibe seed, setts and rhizome with 0.1% PPFM (Pink Pigmented Facultative Methylotrops) or 0.5% KCl (Pottasium Chloride) for 20 minutes to induce drought tolerance of seed materials during establishment stage
  • Foliar spray with 0.1% PPFM - 200 ml / acre / spray or 0.5% KCl - 1 kg / acre / spray to induce drought tolerance of crops during critical growth stages
  • Spraying should be done during the morning or evening hours
  • It can be recommended for all crops
  • Spraying should be done at critical stages of crops at 15 to 20 days intervals
  • Precaution : Do not mix with pesticide / fungicide and fertilizers while spraying
