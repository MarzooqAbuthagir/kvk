B. Disease Preventive Measures

  • Vaccination against Food Mouth Disease (FMD) in cattle, Enterotoxemia and Blue tongue in sheep, Peste Des Petits Ruminants (PPR) in sheep & goat and Ranikhet Disease Vaccine K (RDVK) in poultry is to be done at correct intervals
  • Regular deworming in animals and birds

Vaccination Month Age Intervals
FMD March & September Above 4 months Yearly twice
Enterotoxemia July Above 3 months Yearly once
Blue tongue August Above 4 months Yearly once
PPR June Above 4 months Yearly once
RDVK Round the year 2 Months Once in 2 months
