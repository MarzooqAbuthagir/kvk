4. Institutional Interventions

Custom Hiring Farm Implements
Before NICRA intervention farmers were hiring agricultural instruments from private persons, from long distance and in high cost, since the instruments were not available in correct time for agricultural purpose. After NICRA intervention all necessary machineries are available in the same village itself so that the farmers could do low cost timely agricultural operations.

The Custom hiring farm implements unit is successfully managed by Climate Risk Management Committee (Thalamalayan Pasumai Iyakkam) with 19 members, who have jointly purchased the tractor. So far 1754 farmers were benefited by using these farm implements. The total gross income through the custom hiring centre earned Rs.10,85,216/- from the year 2011 to 2021.

Under NICRA, seed production demonstration on drought tolerant and high yielding varieties on Ground nut, Sorghum, Black gram, Green gram and Red gram crops were taken up for production and availability of quality seeds to NICRA farmers.

So far 250 kg Black gram, 15 kg Red gram, 1000 kg Groundnut and 1000 kg sorghum seeds procured from the seed producing NICRA farmers and made availabale to other farmers.
